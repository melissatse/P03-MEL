Lumen

An online money transfer platform which allows individuals to transfer money between Canada and Iran.

  • Role: Research and Interface design
  • Tools: Figma, Illustrator, & Photoshop
  • Team: Keegan George, Taha Esmael & Seerat Rana
  • Client: Lumen
  • Timeframe: 3 Months

To help Lumen attract and retain customers by enhancing trustworthiness through the process of offering greater transparency.

Lumen visitors are faced with multiple obstacles that might hinder Lumen’s credibility and may prevent them from converting into customers.

Problem

The landing page has no visual representation of what is required to set up an account, exchange rate fluctuations or Lumen’s system. And overall, the landing page fails to provide a personalized introduction, which could be the leading cause for visitors not signing up.

Original UI

The landing page has no visual representation of what is required to set up an account, exchange rate fluctuations or Lumen’s system. And overall, the landing page fails to provide a personalized introduction, which could be the leading cause for visitors not signing up.

We aim to reflect Lumen’s commitment to credibility, transparency, and efficiency among various levels of interactions with existing and future customers.

proposal

Language

Currently, the automatically directs the visitors to Farsi, however, with the new revision, viewers will be given an option to select their desired language upon arrival to the site.

Credibility

Lumen’s credibility is raised as the Fintrac Canada regulation is displayed. Also, the “Learn More” call-to-action allows visitors to conduct more research.

Visitors are also presented with an overview of Lumen’s interface, providing them with an opportunity to get a taste of the system before registration.

Visitors can also view exchange rates and get a quote without registering.

Onboarding

Visitors are also informed about all the steps and documents needed to complete a transaction before registration. This prepares them in advance and sets up for a smooth transaction later on. Having a more humanized representation of the customer testimonies allows for an easier connection for visitors.

More Info

The heatmap reflects real transfers made from different cities in Canada and Iran. This helps demonstrate the validity of Lumen’s customer base.

Furthermore, the about page reveals the story of Lumen’s founders and their purpose for creating Lumen. The revision also shifts the About page to its own separate page, thus, permitting an improved flow for the landing page.

Reflection

First impressions always leave a lasting impact. So, it’s essential to make sure that they are a true reflection of who you are.

The new landing page presents a coherent and consistent reflection of Lumen and illustrates an overview of Lumen and their system. Visitors are presented with humanized testimonials and given an overview of the transaction process beforehand.

To further develop Lumen's new landing page creating a reusable section for new feature updates would help existing customers stay up to date.

I was able to learn new programs in the process of creating Lumen's new landing page. Working in a team with a real-world company also gave me insights on how studio work feels like.
