ParkEasy

a parking reservation app that allows users to see the parking lots around them or where they want to go, their costs, and to reserve it.

  • Role: Research & Interface design
  • Tools: Figma, Illustrator, Photoshop
  • Team: Keegan George & Keeneth Jung
  • Client: Senior interface design course
  • Timeframe: 6 Weeks

Finding parking spots can be especially difficult and many drivers end up wasting a lot of time during their busy schedules circling around lots. Our aim is to make this end part less of an inconvenience.

Our idea is to create a parking reservation application, where you can look at parking lot locations with their rates and then select and reserve your parking spot on a graphical interface.

RESEARCH

Users

Our design domain is transportation and we are specifically focusing on driving. From interviews and surveys, we found there were many issues with driving that our design domain faced, however, parking was an issue brought up several times and a problem that resonated with us personally. Using our findings as a framework, we were able to better understand the struggles of this domain.

Concerns

Regulation concerns could deem the application useless. Thus, we want to ensure how we can implement this idea in a manner that is acceptable. To perform this, we would research parking lots such as Impark to help us assess the situation better

Information Management

To make the idea fully functional, the parking information will need to be completely updated. Therefore, we need to understand the varying durations that parking lots have for vehicle movement. Knowing this could help us to apply time restrictions, if necessary

ParkEasy will assist drivers on their journey and remove a hassle that stems from it.

PROPOSAL

Onboarding

Users enter their location, or the location they want to go and select from the parking lots around them.

Time Selection

Users then select the duration they would like to reserve the parking stall for.

Stall Selection

From there, a map of the selected parking lot is displayed with the real-time availability.

Payment Process

Card payment is selected and processed. A confirmation is presented and reservation is completed.

Extend

Users can also renew the duration of their parking time which removes the need to go back to the parking garage. It also reminds users where they parked their car and can walk them back to their spot.

VALUE PROPOSITION

Spot Insurance

provides a live view into the parking lot and reserve a spot that they would need or want. Ensuring users a parking spot reduces one potential area of worry and allows for trip plans to go much more smoothly.

Time Renewal

removes the need to go back to the parking garage and manually renew parking time. This is an action they can do through the app which allows them to better enjoy their time out.

Digital Guide

reminds the user where they parked and how to get there. The experience of leaving the parking garage becomes just as easy as entering.

REFLECTION

Design for a specific audience. Designing for everyone is designing for noone.

Countless iterations were made to suit the target domain we were designing for. Through this process I was able to gain insight on how important the inital research is.

To further develop ParkEasy, creating an application that could be extended to work on the existing GPS would be convenient.

I was able to learn new programs in the process of creating ParkEasy as well as being able to develop my research skills which can be carried forward to future projects.
